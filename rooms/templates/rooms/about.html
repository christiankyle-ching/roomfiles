About RoomFiles

RoomFiles is a place where you and your colleagues can gather and upload files to collaborate with!

With the ongoing pandemic, more academic institutions are resorting to online services to accomodate their mixed-learning systems.

This website/web-app could be an alternative place for you (with your friends) to store and share your files!

Important note to users!

This website is only a small, non-production type project (for my personal portfolio as a developer), and might still contain some problems.
If you encounter a problem, please contact me explaining what happened, to help me improve this site!

Thank you for using RoomFiles!
